A brief introduction to deepfake technology:

Deepfake technology is a type of artificial intelligence (AI) that creates realistic-looking fake images, videos, and audio recordings. The term is a combination of "deep learning" and "fake". Deepfakes can be used for legitimate purposes, such as in entertainment, video games, and customer support. However, they can also be used to spread misinformation, impersonate people, or commit fraud.
